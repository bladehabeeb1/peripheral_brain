The Peripheral Brain

The Peripheral Brain is a website created and maintained to catalog the vast amount of information provided to Purdue pharmacy students. In an effort to make this information easily accesible and provide a fast reference for rotations and general coursework, this website will be updated as frequently as possible with the most current information provided in the author's curriculum.
